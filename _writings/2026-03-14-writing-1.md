## Context

Briefly describe the context in which this reflection arises.

**Examples:**
- A clinical rotation
- A patient encounter (fully anonymized)
- A public health observation
- A lecture or conference

**Template text:**
This reflection is based on **[clinical rotation / experience / observation]** in **[department or context]**.

---

## Situation or Observation

Describe the situation, case, or idea that triggered the reflection.

Focus on clarity and relevance rather than detail.

**Template text:**
During this experience, I observed **[situation / clinical scenario / systemic issue]**. This raised important questions regarding **[clinical / ethical / public health issue]**.

---

## Key Issue

Identify the central question or problem.

**Examples:**
- Diagnostic difficulty
- Resource limitation
- Patient communication
- Mental health burden
- Health system gaps

**Template text:**
The central issue highlighted in this situation is **[main issue]**.

---

## Analysis

Develop your reasoning. This is the most important section.

You can approach it through:
- clinical reasoning
- public health perspective
- ethical considerations
- system-level constraints

**Guiding questions:**
- Why is this issue important?
- What are the underlying causes?
- What are the risks if it is not addressed?
- How is it usually handled?

**Template text:**
This issue is significant because **[analysis]**. Several factors contribute to it, including **[factors]**. In this context, **[insight or reasoning]**.

---

## Broader Implications

Connect the reflection to a larger context.

**Examples:**
- Burkina Faso healthcare system
- Global health perspective
- Psychiatry / trauma context
- Preventive medicine

**Template text:**
Beyond this specific situation, this reflects broader challenges such as **[systemic issue]**. In the context of **[country or field]**, this is particularly relevant because **[reason]**.

## Key Takeaways

## Personal Reflection

End with a short, personal but professional reflection.

**Template text:**
This experience strengthened my understanding of **[lesson]**. It also highlighted the importance of **[clinical skill / mindset / system awareness]** in medical practice.
